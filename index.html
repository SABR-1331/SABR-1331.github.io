SABR COIN

SABR is a peer-to-peer currency based on hybrid Proof-of-Work/Proof-of-Stake block generation methods that enables instant, near-zero cost payments to anyone in the world. The software is released in a transparent process under the MIT/X11 license that allows independent verification of binaries and their source code.

PRIVACY AND SECURITY

Protect your financial information. Your transactions are private and secure. Every SABR wallet allows private transaction using the 5 series account number. Privacy features support anonymous transactions without the need of master nodes or mixing services when using the 5 series address in the SABR wallet.

SPEED

The SABR blockchain is capable of handling higher transaction volume than its counterpart - Bitcoin. Due to more frequent block generation, the network supports more transactions. As a result, merchants get faster confirmation times, while still having ability to wait for more confirmations when selling bigger ticket items.
